This sub-package holds Wire-Cell Toolkit 3D imaging components.

An informal note on some of the algorithms is in [[https://www.phy.bnl.gov/~bviren/tmp/wctimg/raygrid.pdf][raygrid.pdf]]

Additional topics

- [[file:docs/BlobDepoFill.org][blob depo fill]] gives "true" charge to blobs based on the depos that overlap their volumes.  See also a [[file:docs/talks/blob-depo-fill.org][presentation]].
